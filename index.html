The Ultimate Fluffy Pancake Recipe

Imagine a stack of pancakes so light they practically float off the plate. Their golden-brown surfaces give way to an interior that's airy and tender, each bite dissolving in your mouth like a sweet cloud. Tiny air pockets create a delicate crumb, perfectly absorbing melted butter and a generous drizzle of warm maple syrup that pools invitingly on their pillowy peaks. These aren't just breakfast; they're an experience in pure, unadulterated fluffiness.

Servings: 4

Yield: 8 pancakes

Total time: 25 minutes

Difficult level: Easy

Adjust Yield

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons butter, melted

Nutrition Facts:

  • Calories: 230
  • Fat: 8g
  • Carbs: 33g
  • Protein: 6g

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine milk and vinegar in a medium bowl and set aside for 5 minutes to make sour milk.
  3. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large mixing bowl.
  4. Whisk egg and butter into soured milk
  5. Pour flour mixture into milk mixture and whisk until lumps are gone.
  6. Heat a large skillet over medium heat, and coat it with cooking spray. Pour 1/4 cupfuls of batter onto skillet, and cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, about 2 to 3 minutes.
  7. Flip with a spatula, and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
  8. Enjoy your delicious pancakes!
