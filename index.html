The Ultimate Fluffy Pancake Recipe

Imagine a stack of pancakes so light they practically float off the plate. Their golden-brown surfaces give way to an interior that's airy and tender, each bite dissolving in your mouth like a sweet cloud.

Servings: 4

Yield: 8 pancakes

Total time: 25 minutes

Difficulty level: Easy

Adjust Yield

pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons butter, melted

Nutrition Facts:

  • Calories: 230
  • Fat: 8g
  • Carbs: 33g
  • Protein: 6g

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine milk and vinegar; set aside for 5 minutes.
  3. Combine dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl.
  4. Whisk egg and butter into soured milk.
  5. Pour flour mixture into milk mixture and whisk until lumps are gone.
  6. Cook on a medium-heat skillet for 2-3 minutes per side.
  7. Enjoy your delicious pancakes!
